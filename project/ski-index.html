Welcome

Explore different mountains and see two grades for each: one for overall experience (snow quality, trail variety, scenery, location) and one for price.

Mountains You Can Explore

Whistler, British Columbia

Largest ski resort in North America with quality powder, many trails, and stunning views. Village atmosphere is great; prices are high.

Experience Grade: A+ | Price Grade: C-

Jackson Hole, Wyoming

Steep, challenging terrain for experienced skiers with beautiful Teton views. Lodging and dining are very expensive.

Experience Grade: A | Price Grade: C

Stowe, Vermont

Classic New England resort with solid snow and mixed difficulties. Somewhat high prices, but less than big Western resorts.

Experience Grade: B+ | Price Grade: B

Elk Mountain, Pennsylvania

Well-groomed trails during peak times; can be icy. Shorter lift lines and much lower costs; simpler amenities.

Experience Grade: B- | Price Grade: A-
